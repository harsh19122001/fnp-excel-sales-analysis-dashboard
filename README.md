🌸 Ferns & Petals Sales Analysis Dashboard
Project Overview

This project analyzes sales data from Ferns & Petals (FNP) to uncover insights related to revenue performance, customer behavior, product demand, delivery performance, and seasonal sales trends.

The objective was to transform raw business data into actionable insights that support decision-making and improve overall business performance.

🎯 Business Problem

Ferns & Petals wanted to answer several important business questions:

Which occasions generate the highest revenue?
Which products are the strongest revenue contributors?
Which cities place the highest number of orders?
How much do customers spend on average?
How does sales performance change throughout the year?
Which product categories perform best?
How efficient is the delivery process?

Answering these questions helps the business improve marketing effectiveness, inventory planning, customer experience, and sales performance.
